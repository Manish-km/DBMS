Welcome To Chiguru 2021

Chiguru is the inter-college fest of Cambridge Institute of Technology, KR puram. It is the celebration of all forms of cultural and technical talents. Students from different colleges all over Bangalore have been actively participating in Chiguru since past years and we expect the same participation this year too. This year Chiguru 2k21 is being conducted on 18th and 19th of March and the venue is at the main campus of Cambridge Institute of Technology, KR puram. The fest will be flagged off on 18th at 11:00am by our beloved chairman Sir DK Mohan and our respected principal Dr.Suresh L with the presence of other dignitaries. Our Guest of Honour the famous kanada actor Yash will be arriving to our fest on the 18th. The cultural competitions will begin from 11 am on 18th and will also be carried on the next day. The concert headed by famous artists will begin by 6pm of the second day of the festival.
